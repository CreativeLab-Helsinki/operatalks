Opera on Fridays

An in-depth introduction to opera as an art form, theater, and cultural phenomenon. We explore music, dramaturgy, and history through the lens of one of the world's most complex stage arts.

The Grand Tour

The program includes an exclusive guided tour of the National Finnish Opera and Ballet (Helsinki), plus organized group visits to live performances throughout the season.

The Curriculum

  • Historical Era Workshops
  • Analysis of Musical Excerpts
  • Dramaturgy & Stagecraft
  • Joint Performance Visits
  • National Opera Backstage Tour
